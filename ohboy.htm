Oh Boy! Waffles


2 1/2 CupsFlour
4 TeaspoonsBaking Powder
3/4 TeaspoonSalt
1 1/2 TablespoonsSugar
2Beaten Eggs
2 1/4 CupsMilk
3/4 CupMelted Shortening or Salad Oil

Sift dry ingredients. Combine eggs, milk and shortening. Combine liquid and dry ingredients just before baking; beat til smooth. This is a thin batter. Bake in hot waffle iron. Makes 10 waffles. Serve with Honey-Butter Sauce.
