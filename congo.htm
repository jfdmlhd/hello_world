Congo Bars


1 BoxBrown Sugar
1 1/2 Sticks(3/4 Cup)Butter(Melted)
3Eggs
2 3/4 CupsFlour
2 teaspoonsBaking Powder
1/4 teaspoonSalt
1 CupChopped Pecans
1 Small Pkg (8 oz)Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

Mix brown sugar, butter, and eggs until creamy. Then mix flour, baking powder and salt in separate bowl. Add to butter-sugar mixture a little at a time until all is added. You may have to mix with spoon because it will get very stiff. Add nuts and chocolate chips last. Cook in greased pan at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes.
