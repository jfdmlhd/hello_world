Peanut-butter Crisscrosses


1 cupshortening
1 cupgranulated sugar
1 cupbrown sugar
1 teaspoonvanilla
1 cuppeanut butter
3 cupssifted enriched flour
2 teaspoonsbaking soda
dashsalt

Thoroughly cream shortening, sugars, and vanilla. Add eggs; beat thoroughly. Stir in peanut butter. Sift dry ingredients; stir into creamed mixture.

Form into tiny balls; place on ungreased cookie sheet. Press with back of fork to make crisscross. Dough may also be rolled and cut if desired.

Bake in moderate oven (375 degrees) 10 minutes. Makes 8 dozen cookies.
