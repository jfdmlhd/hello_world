Salman Croquets


116oz can of salmon
2eggs
1pkg crackers crushed
approx 1 cupcooked rice
approx 1/2 cupevaporated milk
1/4 teaspoonblack pepper

Drain salmon. Mix with rice and 1 egg. Add crushed crackers until it is a consistency such that it can be rolled into little balls. Roll all of mixture into balls.

Mix evaporated milk, egg, and pepper in one container. In another container put remainder of crushed crackers. Take each of the croquettes, dip them in egg mixture and then immediately into crackers. After they have all been dipped and covered in crushed crackers begin placing them in a greased frying pan on medium heat. Turn as they cook so that they are brown on all sides. Watch carefully to avoid burning.
