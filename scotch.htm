Scotch Teas


1/2 cupButter or Margarine
1 cupBrown Sugar
2 cupsquick-cooking rolled oats
1/4 teaspoonSalt
1 teaspoonBaking Powder

Combine butter and sugar in saucepan; cook and stir till butter melts. Stir in remaining ingredients; mix well. Pour into greased 8x8x2-inch pan. Bake in 350 degree oven 20 to 25 minutes. Cool; cut into bars. Makes 2 dozen.
